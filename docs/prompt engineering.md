# 🗺️ Mindanao Regional Development: Prompt Engineering Playbook
**Role:** Digital Solutions Architect  
**Audience:** LGU Technical Working Group (Davao del Norte)  
**Objective:** Lock AI systems into strict geographic, structural, and cultural contexts to eliminate generic, Western-centric content and optimize localized agricultural logistics.

---

### 🌾 The Davao del Norte Agro-Logistics Prompt System

#### 1. System Prompt Template (V3 - Final Optimized)
> "Act as a Senior Agricultural Logistics Advisor specializing in Southern Mindanao's supply chains, specifically the Davao del Norte corridor (Tagum City, Panabo, and Sto. Tomas). Your objective is to draft a 300-word logistical contingency and routing brief for local banana and cacao smallholder transport cooperatives facing severe local infrastructure delays.
>
> **Context:** Smallholders are experiencing prolonged cargo spoilage and transit delays along the Sayre Highway and local arterial farm-to-market roads due to localized flooding and unpredictable weather patterns affecting key consolidation hubs.
>
> **Constraints:** Use an urgent yet professional, community-centered tone. Do NOT reference global trade indexes or broad international shipping laws irrelevant to local small farms. Focus exclusively on local provincial arterial roads, regional cold storage hubs, and municipal cooperative networks. Do not use corporate or Western-centric logistical jargon.
>
> **Format:** Output in clear Markdown with exactly three actionable steps under the heading '### Emergency Interventions'."

---

#### 2. Prompt Battle Ledger

| Version | Prompt Modifier Added | Output Quality Reflection |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **V1** | "Write a plan for delayed farms in Davao." | **Failed.** Too broad. The AI generated generic international shipping laws and high-level macroeconomics completely irrelevant to small local farms. |
| **V2** | Added specific regional persona, named specific highways, and designated localized banana/cacao farm constraints. | **Better.** Geographically accurate, but the language became overly academic, dense, and impractical for local farm cooperatives to quickly implement. |
| **V3** | Added a strict 300-word execution limit, banned corporate jargon, and forced a strict markdown format with an exact action-heading constraint. | **Target Hit.** Direct, highly actionable, and hyper-localized to Mindanao logistics and municipal reality. |
